Collect coins, dodge meteors — at 25 score, Zeus takes over. At 50, Poseidon emerges from the depths.
How to play
  • Flap: Space (or tap/click)
  • Movement: only vertical (gravity pulls you down)
  • Lives: 3 hearts — hits reduce hearts and briefly stun you
  • Coin: +1 score, respawns after 250ms at a random visible Y
  • Zeus: at score 25 meteors vanish; lightning charges stun longer
  • Poseidon: at score 50 the seas rise — fly over rolling waves and dodge tridents
  • Restart: R (or the Restart button)
